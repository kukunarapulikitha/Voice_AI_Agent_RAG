# Pump X200 — Operator & Maintenance Manual

## 1. Overview
The Pump X200 is an industrial water pump designed for continuous-duty operation in
municipal and light-industrial settings. It is a single-stage centrifugal pump with a
cast-iron housing and a stainless-steel impeller.

## 2. Technical Specifications
- Maximum operating pressure: 150 psi
- Maximum flow rate: 220 gallons per minute
- Rated power: 5 horsepower
- Supply voltage: 230 volts, single phase
- Maximum fluid temperature: 90 degrees Celsius
- Inlet / outlet diameter: 2 inches
- Net weight: 41 kilograms

## 3. Consumables and Pricing
- Replacement inlet filter: 45 dollars
- Replacement mechanical seal kit: 120 dollars
- Impeller replacement: 310 dollars
- Recommended lubricant (1 liter): 18 dollars

## 4. Overload Reset Procedure
If the pump trips on thermal overload, follow these steps:
1. Switch the main power to the off position and wait five minutes for the motor to cool.
2. Press and hold the red reset button on the control box for three seconds.
3. Verify the inlet valve is fully open so the pump is not running dry.
4. Switch the main power back on and confirm the green status light is steady.

## 5. Routine Maintenance Schedule
- Inspect and clean the inlet filter every 30 days.
- Replace the mechanical seal kit every 12 months or after 4000 operating hours.
- Check impeller clearance every 6 months.
- Lubricate the motor bearings every 3 months.

## 6. Safety
Always disconnect power before servicing. Do not operate the pump above 150 psi or above
90 degrees Celsius fluid temperature, as this may damage the seal and void safe operation.

## 7. Troubleshooting
- Low flow: check for a clogged inlet filter or a closed inlet valve.
- Excessive vibration: check impeller balance and bearing wear.
- Pump will not start: verify power supply and check the thermal overload (see section 4).
